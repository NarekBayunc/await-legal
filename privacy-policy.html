Privacy Policy

Last updated: 28.05.2026

This policy explains how Await handles your data.

1. Local storage

Await stores your events, milestones, settings, language, theme, Life Progress data, profile name, date of birth, and selected images locally on your device.

2. Servers and sale of data

At this time, Await does not send your user-created events, countdowns, milestones, dates, images, or Life Progress data to our own server.

Await does not sell your personal data.

3. Permissions

The app may request permissions for the following features:

- Notifications and reminders: to send local reminders about events and milestones. On Android, the app may use system exact alarms for exact reminders.
- Calendar: to access device calendars and import events or milestones. Depending on the OS, this may appear as full calendar access.
- Photos/gallery: to select an image or avatar and to save share images.
- Ads: to show advertising where available in the app.

4. Advertising and Google AdMob

Await may show ads using Google AdMob.

AdMob is a third-party advertising service. Google/AdMob may collect or process data such as device identifiers, ad interactions, diagnostics, approximate location, and other data depending on user, device, and account settings.

Await does not send your user-created events or countdowns to AdMob. Google's own privacy policies apply to data processed by AdMob.

Where available, you can control personalized ads through your device or Google account ad settings.

5. Data usage

Your data is used only to make the app features work.

6. Data deletion

You can delete data inside the app or remove the app from your device. When the app is deleted, local data may be removed by your device system.

7. Security

We try to use a safe approach to data storage. But because data is stored on your device, security also depends on your phone and system protection.

8. Children

Await is not intended to collect data from children. If you are under the age of majority, use the app with permission from a parent or guardian.

9. Policy changes

This policy may be updated. The new version will be available inside the app.
